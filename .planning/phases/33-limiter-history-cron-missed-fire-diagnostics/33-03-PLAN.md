# 33-03 Plan: Proof and Retention Boundaries

## Goal

Close proof and retention-boundary behavior for limiter and cron history so operators see explicit unknown or partial-evidence states when data is incomplete.

## Implementation

- Extend `Forensics.bundle/2` routing for limiter and cron stable selectors.
- Render limiter and cron history through the same read models in summary pages and full forensic bundles.
- Preserve Phase 32 completeness vocabulary: complete, partial evidence, history unavailable, and unknown.
- Add LiveView and projection tests for selector continuity, summary handoffs, and incomplete-evidence states.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Limiter and cron forensic destinations survive refresh/remount from stable selectors.
- Summary cards and full forensic bundles agree because they share read models.
- Missing history is explicit and never displayed as certainty.
- Audit remains a linked evidence destination, not the primary history store.
