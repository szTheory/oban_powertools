**Status:** ✅ SHIPPED 2026-05-27

## Overview

Oban Powertools v1.4 deepens the now-stable native control plane into a stronger day-2 operations surface. The milestone adds durable forensic timelines, limiter and cron history, evidence-bundle drilldowns, runbook-guided remediation, and support-truthful host integration seams without reopening generic queue-dashboard scope or freezing machine-facing automation contracts too early.

## Milestone Summary

**Key Decisions:**

- Activate the default arc candidate `v1.4 Operator Forensics & SRE Runbooks` without resetting phase numbering; continue at phases 32-42.
- Keep the milestone focused on investigative leverage, historical context, and runbook guidance rather than a native generic queue-dashboard rewrite.
- Treat alerting and escalation as explicit host-owned seams; Powertools may explain and hook into them but should not imply first-party ownership of downstream delivery truth.
- Reuse the v1.3 control-plane vocabulary everywhere so forensics and runbooks strengthen the existing operator contract instead of creating a parallel language.
- Require explicit partial-evidence and unknown-state handling wherever historical data can be absent, retained away, or bridge-only.
- After milestone audit gaps, add dedicated closure phases for verification backfill (37), docs/example-host closure (38), and CI continuity proof wiring (39).
- After re-audit surfaced remaining debt, add dedicated gap-closure phases for manual acceptance closure (40), advisory hardening (41), and Nyquist compliance sweep (42).
- Phase 36 is a reconciliation umbrella with additive chronology only: `DOC-05` closure stays in Phase 38, `VER-04` closure stays in Phase 39.

**Issues Resolved:**

- Orphaned FRN-01/02/03 and OPS-01/02 verification artifacts (no `32-VERIFICATION.md` or `33-VERIFICATION.md`) — resolved by Phase 37 backfill.
- DOC-05 unsatisfied (broken docs/example-host forensics journey) — resolved by Phase 38 canonical closure.
- VER-04 unsatisfied (no CI lane wiring for continuity suites) — resolved by Phase 39 with 4 named merge-blocking lanes.
- OPS-03, RNB-01, RNB-02 open manual acceptance gates — resolved by Phase 40 automated proxy tests.
- WR-01/WR-02 advisory hardening debt (URL selector reliability, dynamic atom normalization) — resolved by Phase 41.
- Nyquist validation gaps in phases 33, 34, 38, 39 — resolved by Phase 42 sweep.

**Technical Debt Incurred:**

- Continuity proof closure scoped to targeted reruns for phases 32 and 33; repo-wide confidence covered by Phase 39 CI lanes.
- Generic queue dashboard and machine-facing API/CLI automation surfaces deferred to v1.5+ (API-02, QRY-01, ALR-01).
