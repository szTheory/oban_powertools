Close gap REL-04 by making four surgical, non-conflicting edits across three files so the `verify-published` CI job completes end-to-end:

- **D-01:** Insert a `rm -f` step in the `verify-published` job that deletes the two committed scaffold files (`oban_powertools_auth.ex`, `oban_powertools_display_policy.ex`) before the installer runs, so Igniter generates them fresh and produces the 6 required migration files instead of emitting `:error issues` and skipping migration generation.
